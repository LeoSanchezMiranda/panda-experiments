CULTURAL

Visit the Zombie Museums, art exhibits and trophy galleries. We provide some of the most complete collections for zombie art. All kinds of creations made by and/or with zombies.

STORES

Go crazy in one of our several stores. Everything that has to do with zombies, from souvenirs to furniture. Stores are not only for hunters.

HISTORY

Real survivors of the First Outbreak tell their story. Part of the cultural area that focus on the events that originated the zombie hunting sport. Live recreations of the first Outbreak twice a year.

TOURS TO GROUND ZERO

If you already know the events of how the zombie apocalypse was conceived, it’s time to experience the real thing (with some extra precautions). See the live (or in this case dead) evidence and get your camera ready for this event. (No shooting allowed during the tour, These zombies are not part of the hunted group)

BAR

Either if you need to get some motivation or want to calm down after seeing your buddy eaten after a wrong move. Great ambience and weekend specials.

RESTAURANTS

Visit our family restaurant with special amenities for children, play area, entertainers and a great view. Get a different experience in our elegant top of the line Z-Terrace, where you can see the zombies in their natural habitat. Everything inside the safety of our wall-less fail safe bio dome. If you feel adventurous you can always try the “Fast Food” Challenge.

BIO LAB VISITS

If you ever wondered how we get those cyber-zombies or some of the special creepy mutations, this is your chance to understand the process and see the beginning of a new creation. You’ll have the chance to create your own creepy mutation in a safe environment (not like those Umbrella scientists).

Specials for NRA members

We have the best options for you. From explosive tip arrows to the new mini patriot grenade luncher. Ask for our mechanical companion.
